Prophet Forecasting Model - FOODS_1 Department¶

Objective¶

Build a time-series forecasting model using Facebook Prophet to predict future retail demand for the FOODS_1 department.

Steps Performed¶

Data Preparation¶

  • Loaded feature engineered FOODS_1 dataset
  • Aggregated sales at daily level
  • Converted dataset into Prophet format:
    • ds = Date
    • y = Sales

Exploratory Analysis¶

  • Analyzed yearly sales trend
  • Analyzed monthly sales trend
  • Identified seasonality patterns
  • Verified weekend effect
  • Confirmed suitability for time-series forecasting

Train-Test Split¶

  • Train Data: 1883 days
  • Test Data: 30 days
